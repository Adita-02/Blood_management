Mobile Blood Donation Initiatives Reaching Every Corner.

Our 25 mobile units have traveled over 5,000 miles in 2025 alone, bringing life-saving donation opportunities to corporate offices, college campuses, and rural communities. Through innovative community partnerships and awareness campaigns, we've increased first-time donors by 40% in underserved areas.

2025 Outreach Milestones

Quarterly Progress

Q1 Innovation: Launched our on-site blood drives hosted at major corporations with our mobile donation buses. Employee participation increased 7% through our incentive programs and executive health check packages.

Q2 Expansion: Established 10 new rural collection centers equipped with solar-powered refrigeration units. These centers now serve approximately 120 remote communities, reducing travel time for donors by an average of 2.5 hours.

Q3 Campus Connect: University participation rate with our semester-long donor education program. Student donor retention increased through exam-time blood requirements and health awareness festivals.

Q4 Community Engagement: Hosted our largest annual donor recognition event to date, honoring 450+ dedicated volunteers across 8 Division and main district of them with our LifeSaver Awards program.
